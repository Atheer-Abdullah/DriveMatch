Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you register as a driver on DriveMatch, we collect the following information: your full name, email address, phone number, and national ID or Iqama number. We also collect details about your vehicle including make, model, year, color, license plate, and car photo.

2. Identity and Verification Documents

To verify your identity and ensure the safety of our passengers, we collect photos of your ID or Iqama (front and back) and your driver's license. These documents are reviewed by our team and are not shared publicly. Only authorized DriveMatch staff can access your verification documents.

3. Location and Routes

We store the routes and neighborhoods you make available for booking. This information is shown to passengers searching for a driver on your routes.

4. Payments and Invoices

Booking payments made by passengers are recorded in the system. Your bank account details (if provided for payout settings) are stored securely and used only to process your earnings payouts. We do not store full card numbers — only the last 4 digits for reference.

5. Ratings and Reviews

Passengers can rate your service after completing a booking. Ratings and comments are stored and may be shown on your public driver profile to help passengers make informed decisions.

6. Support Tickets and Complaints

When you submit a support ticket or a complaint is filed against you, the content of that ticket is visible to DriveMatch support staff. We use this information to resolve disputes and improve our service.

7. Data Protection

Your password is securely hashed and is never stored in plain text. All uploaded documents are stored on our secure server. We take reasonable measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or request deletion of your personal data. To exercise these rights, please contact our support team.

9. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, please contact us at support@drivematch.com or use our support page.
