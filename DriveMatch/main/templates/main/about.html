About DriveMatch

Connecting passengers with verified monthly drivers in Riyadh

What is DriveMatch?

DriveMatch is a platform that connects passengers with verified professional drivers on a monthly subscription basis. Instead of booking individual rides, passengers subscribe to a reliable daily driver who covers their regular route — to work, school, or any other destination — every day of the month.

How It Works

1
Search

Passengers search for verified drivers by route, time, and price.

2
Subscribe

Book a driver for 1, 2, 3, 6, or 12 months. A secure contract is created automatically.

3
Ride

Your driver picks you up daily on the agreed route and schedule.

Verified Drivers

All drivers on DriveMatch go through a verification process before appearing in search results:

  • National ID or Iqama verification
  • Valid driver's license confirmation
  • Vehicle registration and inspection
  • Admin review and approval

Only approved drivers can receive booking requests.

Safe Contracts and Support

Every booking generates a digital contract that defines the route, schedule, price, and cancellation terms. Our support team is available to resolve disputes, process refunds, and assist with any service issues.
