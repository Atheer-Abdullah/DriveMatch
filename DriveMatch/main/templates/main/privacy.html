Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information including your name, email address, phone number, national ID, and vehicle information when you register as a driver or passenger.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to provide the platform service, verify driver identity, process bookings and payments, and send important notifications.

3. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal data. Passwords are securely hashed and documents are stored securely.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. Driver and passenger information is shared between each other only as necessary to complete a booking.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information by contacting our support team.

6. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, please contact us at support@drivematch.com.
